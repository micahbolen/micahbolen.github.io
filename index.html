Escher Puzzle


Open in new tab
Source Code

A WebGL 3d visualization inspired by the Escher Vault that appears in the TV show, Warehouse 13.

Three.js is used to ease the burden of setting up the scene and rendering all models, shaders, lighting, filters, and textures. However, much of the underlying logic for the visualization is original work.
